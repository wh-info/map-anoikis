anoikis.info is an interactive map of EVE Online's wormhole space (Anoikis) featuring 2,604 wormhole systems with a live killfeed and per-system intel.

Search for systems, browse their details, analyze activity patterns, and explore a solar-system orrery.
Active fights are detected and marked on the map. Focus the killfeed on any system to watch the action live, or check intel for the full record and activity history.

Stay informed about everything happening across wormhole space.

Occasionally the backend may restart for updates or maintenance. The killfeed will be empty after a restart and will populate as new kills come in.

You can support this project by leaving feedback, buying me a coffee, or simply sending me an o7 in-game :)
